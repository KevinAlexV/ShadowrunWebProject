Disc.

Who am I? Disc. Disc is the name. Many people wonder where I come from. Heh. I'll never tell. I like the mystery. All that needs to be said, is that I come from a place that gives plenty of combat experience. We also used to wear uniforms. Those were kind of fun. It isn't my style anymore though. I guess a coping mechanism for such a grueling part of my life is simply to take my frustrations to the streets. It gives me some way of forgetting about the past, yet using that experience for my own benefit. So I am what people call a Shadowrunner. They can laugh at my voice all they want, but they don't know the true potential that I carry. I have more kills under my belt then I care to count. My past no longer haunts me like it once did, but not telling others gives me the benefit of the doubt. It also ensures that my past can stay buried, alongside the rest of those who once knew me. I am not that person anymore, they died long ago. Now, I am Disc. Disc, the Street Samurai
