This is the world of Shadowrun

A world where magic and technology co-exist, Orks and Elfs mingle, Fighters and Hackers team up. This world's vast nature is only limited by your own imagination. However, if you wish to venture forth into the worlds written out by others, you need a character.

The characters that exist here are binded to a page, determined through the act of rolling a die. A 6 sided die, to be precise. This site has some pre-made characters for you to use, based on three of the games classes: The Decker, Street Samurai, and the Combat Adept.

Decker

The Technological Wizard

  • Enter the 3D virtual-reality of the Matrix
  • Tap into phone calls at will
  • Hack your surroundings to change the tide of a battle

Street Samurai

The Physical Combat Specialist

  • Wield a variety of weapons to take down foes in a heart-beat
  • Use cyber or bioware to enhance your combat abilities
  • Strength is your ally, use it to solve your problems

Combat Mage

The Magic wielding Combatant

  • Use Spells to shred through enemies
  • Channel some Magic to enhance your abilities
  • Perform the impossible using the mystic arts

Fifth Edition

Shadowrun, Fifth Edition is the newest version of this role-playing game. With rules for character creation, magic, combat, Matrix hacking, rigging, and more, you have everything you need to face the challenges of the Sixth World.

This edition of Shadowrun takes place in 2075, a future long after an event called the 'awakening'. That event cause the world of technology to collide with the world of magic. The age when Elfs began to materialize, and where magic and dragons were no longer fiction.
